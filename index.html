SSRI Defense Cases (overview)

In rare cases, defendants have argued medication side effects contributed to impaired judgment. Courts evaluate these claims case-by-case; raising an argument does not equal acceptance.

Why Zoloft & Prozac are often mentioned

  • High name recognition and widespread prescribing.
  • Some report early “activation” (restlessness, agitation) after starting or changing dose.
  • Media narratives can oversimplify complex contexts (co-use, sleep loss, untreated illness).

Teaching note

This panel is for sociological discussion (media framing, stigma, pharmaceuticalization), not for proving causation in any one case.
